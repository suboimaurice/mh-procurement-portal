How Our Procurement Portal Works

Streamlining the acquisition of medical supplies and services for efficient outpatient care.

1. Browse Categories

Departments select required medical supplies, lab equipment, pharmaceuticals, or services from organized categories.

2. Add To Order

Specify quantity and add needed items to your order list for consolidated procurement.

3. Review & Submit

Review the compiled order, download a PDF form if needed, and submit for procurement processing.

Our Commitment to You

Our portal aims to increase efficiency, transparency, and quality assurance through every step of the procurement cycle, providing 24/7 assistance and same day delivery for urgent needs.
